Vllang – VR Language Learning Platform

Project Overview

Vllang is a virtual reality language immersion platform. It lets users step into cultural replicas of countries like France, Japan, or Spain — enabling peer interaction and vocabulary learning in realistic 3D environments.

Technology Stack
  • Unity (VR Toolkit, C#)
  • Voice Recognition APIs
  • Photon/NativeWebSocket for multiplayer
Key Features
  • Scenario-based dialogues (e.g., shopping in Paris)
  • Live multiplayer rooms for practice
  • Grammar hints and visual feedback
